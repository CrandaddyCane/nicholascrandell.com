Work

Technology only works in a business environment when its users are happy with it.

Throughout my career, I have worked at the intersection of business, technology, data, and operations. While my titles have ranged from Business Manager and Technical Account Manager to Client Relations Manager and Digital Project Manager, the common thread has remained the same: helping organizations implement technology, improve processes, and create meaningful business outcomes.

I enjoy translating between technical teams, operational stakeholders, leadership, vendors, and customers. Whether evaluating software, implementing new systems, building reporting solutions, improving digital experiences, or managing complex projects, I focus on helping organizations navigate change successfully and maximize the value of their technology investments.

The Common Thread

My career has never followed a single discipline. Instead, it has been built around understanding how organizations work and helping them improve through technology, data, and process optimization.

Experience in accounting taught me how businesses measure success. Business intelligence taught me how to uncover insights. Operations management taught me how organizations function.
