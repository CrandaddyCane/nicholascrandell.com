About Me

I'm a problem solver at heart.

I was raised in the Pacific Northwest, where I've spent most of my life, though my family roots trace back to the Midwest. Growing up between those influences helped shape the way I approach both life and work: practical, collaborative, and focused on finding solutions that create real value.


Professionally, I've built a career at the intersection of business, technology, and people. I enjoy understanding how systems work, identifying opportunities for improvement, and helping organizations navigate change.

Whether the challenge involves technology, data, operations, or process improvement, I'm motivated by the opportunity to make complex problems easier to understand and solve.

This website serves as a place to share my professional work, technical projects, and ongoing journey as a lifelong learner.
